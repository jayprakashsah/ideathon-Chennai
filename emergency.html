Emergency Guides

Step-by-step instructions for common emergencies

Heart Attack

1

Call emergency services immediately

2

Have the person sit down and rest

3

If prescribed, help them take nitroglycerin

4

Give aspirin if not allergic (chew 325mg)

5

Begin CPR if person becomes unresponsive

Choking

1

Ask "Are you choking?" - if they can't speak, act

2

Stand behind them, wrap arms around waist

3

Make a fist, place thumb-side above navel

4

Grasp fist with other hand, give quick upward thrusts

5

Continue until object is expelled or person becomes unresponsive

Stroke (FAST)

F

Face - Ask to smile. Does one side droop?

A

Arms - Ask to raise both arms. Does one drift down?

S

Speech - Ask to repeat a simple phrase. Is speech slurred?

T

Time - If any symptoms, call 911 immediately

!

Note the time symptoms first appeared

Severe Bleeding

1

Call emergency services immediately

2

Remove clothing to see wound

3

Apply direct pressure with clean cloth

4

If blood soaks through, add more cloth (don't remove)

5

Elevate injured area above heart if possible
