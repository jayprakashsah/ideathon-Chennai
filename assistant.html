CPR (Cardiopulmonary Resuscitation)

1

Check responsiveness and call for help

2

Place heel of hand on center of chest

3

Push hard and fast (100-120 compressions/min)

Choking - Heimlich Maneuver

1

Ask "Are you choking?" - if they can't speak, act

2

Stand behind, wrap arms around waist

3

Give quick upward abdominal thrusts

Burn Treatment

1

Cool burn with running water (10-15 min)

2

Remove tight items before swelling

3

Cover with sterile non-stick dressing
